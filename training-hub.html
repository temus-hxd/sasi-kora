Lesson 1.1: SGO Mission and Volunteer Role

Welcome to the SGO Volunteer Training Hub! In this lesson, you'll learn about the Silver Generation Office's mission and your important role as a volunteer ambassador.

The Silver Generation Office was established in 2014 and merged with the Agency for Integrated Care in 2018. As a Silver Generation Ambassador (SGA), you'll conduct door-to-door Preventive Health Visits with seniors aged 60 and above, helping them access important health services and government schemes.

What are the key goals of a Preventive Health Visit?

Great question! The key goals of a Preventive Health Visit include:

  • Build rapport and trust with seniors in the community
  • Identify seniors who may need support services
  • Educate seniors about available government schemes and healthcare benefits
  • Encourage healthy aging behaviors and regular health screenings

Visits typically last up to 45 minutes and occur at least once every 18 months per household. This gives you time to have meaningful conversations and assess the senior's needs.
